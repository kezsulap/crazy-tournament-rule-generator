# EN 

On this board there are 7 possible denominations to bid, from lowest to highest ranked:
- ♣s
- ♦s
- ♥s
- no ♥s (scores like play in a major suit)
- ♠s
- no trump
- no tricks (scores like notrump)

Contract of X NO TRICKS is to force opponents to make at least 6 + X tricks, getting your 
opponents to take more counts like overtricks, to take fewer like undertricks

Contract of X NO HEARTS is to force your opponent to take tricks containing at least 6 + X hearts while playing in no trump, if this is the chosen contract leading ♥s is not allowed unless either player has no cards of other suits left
or a heart has been lead or discarded previously.
