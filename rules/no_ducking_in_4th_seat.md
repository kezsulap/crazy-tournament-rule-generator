# EN 

After 3 cards have been played to the trick if the card which is so far winning was played by one of the opponents of the 4th player and that player is able to win the trick, they are required to do so.
If they have multiple cards that can win, any of them can be played.

# PL

Po zagraniu 3 kart w lewie, jeśli aktualnie lewę bierze jeden z przeciwników czwartego gracza i ma on możliwość wzięcia tej lewy, to musi to zrobić. Jeśli ma więcej niż jedną kartę, którą może wziąć, to
może wybrać dowolną.
